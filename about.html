THANK YOU FOR VIEWING MY WEBSITE!

I really hope you enjoyed my projects and content. That's what keeps me driven to make more and write more and share more.

This website started out as a fun idea to brush up on my front end skills but little did I know about how invested I would get down the line to make this so personal and so engaging. It serves as a platform for my ideas to be seen, read and heard and it will go through changes and updates.

As a designer who is now moving into software engineering, I have experience in two very different, yet innately overlapping fields - and I have a lot to talk about regarding that. I can relate with people who are new to code and yet so fascinated by it. I can also relate to people in design that are growing more and more aware of their reliance on technology, whether it is surface modeling in CAD or algorithmic design. The website is only just incipient at the moment but I plan on sharing a lot of cool projects and articles on it. I see this as an ongoing project that I keep adding more and more to.

If you like what you see here or want to say hi, please feel free to contact me here.
