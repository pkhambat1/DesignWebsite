Tipsy Whiskey Glass

A contemporary spin (no pun intended) on the standard whiskey glass that makes it hard to take your eyes off your drink. It can only rest at a seemingly precarious angle, and it engages its user to try and tip it and watch it wobble.

The glass has a unique bottom-seeking weight dynamic in the hand

... And it wobbles on the table!

Buckle End Table

A luxuriously built end table, made from maple wood and cold-rolled steel, with a twist - it flat packs with no tools required.

The end table conviniently comes apart into 5 parts in total. This makes it convinient for transport or shipping, as well as during moving residences.

Assembly in action

The bottom ring creates pulls the 3 legs towards the center and tension locks the legs into the wooden top.
